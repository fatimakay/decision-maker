What is Random Decision Maker?

The random decision maker is a tool to help you make your day to day decisions. Whether it is deciding which chore to complete first, or trying to choose what to have for lunch, use this tool to solve your dilemmas. All you need to do is to put all your choices in the box above, seperated by commas. After that just hit "Enter" and let us choose for you! The selected choice will be highlighted once the tool has finished running.


Warning! decision maker is a fun tool for making trivial decisions. Please do not make important life-changing choices using this tool.
